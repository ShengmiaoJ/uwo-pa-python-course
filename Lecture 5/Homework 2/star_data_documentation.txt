Columns:
- Name - Name of the star
- RA - Right Ascension in degrees, ICRS coord. (J2000)
- Dec - Declination in degrees, ICRS coord. (J2000)
- pa_RA - Proper motion in right ascension, in miliarcseconds per year
- pa_dec - Proper motion in declination, in miliarcseconds per year
- Vr - radial velocity in kilometers per second, a positive value means that the star is moving away from us
- parallax - parallax of the star in miliarcseconds, HINT: to convert the parallax to the distance in parsecs, use: d = 1/parallax

Other values:
1 parsec = 3.0857e13 km
1 parsec = 2.0626481e5 AU
1 parsec = 3.26156 ly
1 AU = 1.495978707e8 km
1 ly = 9.4607304725808e12 km
